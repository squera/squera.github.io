Short Bio


I was born in Ravenna, Italy in 1986.
I got my bachelor and master degree (theoretical curriculum) from the University of Bologna. I did my bachelor thesis with Danilo Montesi and my master one jointly with Davide Sangiorgi and Dave Clarke.
I did my Ph.D. in Leuven with Dave Clarke and (after he left) Frank Piessens. You can find my thesis in this page (2015).
Afterwards, I spent 2 years as a postdoc at MPI-SWS in Saarbrücken with Deepak Garg.
In 2017 I joined the Cispa-Stanford programme still in Saarbrücken.
In 2018 I started my visit as an assistant professor at Stanford University in the Security Lab where John Mitchell was my mentor.
In 2021 I became junior research group leader at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security.
In 2022 I won a RLM grant and started my tenure-track professorship (RTD-B) at the DISI at the University of Trento.
